Hello, W-.. Web!

This is your website.

It's minimal and bland, but be careful!

It's also got a lot going for it:

  • Lightweight and effective
  • Compatible with all different screens and browsers
  • Accessible for all of your visitors

You probably still want to change it

And you probably should, but by making it more personal and (hopefully) beautiful, you may just sacrifice what you already had.

“Good design is as little design as possible.”
- Dieter Rams

So be careful.

Sometimes it's better to be basic.

Minimal, not default.

Make sure it is truly better. You may think the margins are too large, and for desktop: you may just be right! On a phone, you may just have to scroll. But this website isn't sacrificing for mobile. You also aren't a giraffe; it's a whole lot easier to read when it isn't across the entire breadth of the screen or in the corners and edges
